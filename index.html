ProofVisit: Secure Your Presence

Cryptographic proof of business visits using QR codes and secure, privacy-first verification.

In Development - 75% Complete

Core Features

Built for security, simplicity, and privacy.

Cryptographic Security

Every visit generates a tamper-proof SHA-256 hash using device ID, timestamp, and verification word.

QR Code Scanning

Simple camera-based QR scanning with real-time business lookup from Firestore database.

Business Analytics

Real-time visit tracking, trend analysis, and time-based insights with interactive charts.

Privacy First

Device-based anonymity with no personal data collection or account requirements for customers.

Auto Word Rotation

Automatic verification word rotation with configurable intervals and custom word lists.

Visit History

Customers can share cryptographic proofs of loyalty with businesses for verification.

How It Works

A simple and secure process from start to finish.

1

Business Setup

Business owners create QR codes for their locations and set verification words.

2

Customer Scan

Customers scan QR codes and ask staff for the current verification word.

3

Cryptographic Proof

The app generates a secure hash combining visit details for tamper-proof verification.

4

Analytics & History

Both parties can verify visits and track patterns without compromising privacy.
