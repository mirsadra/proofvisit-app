Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 4 February 2026

1. Our "Privacy by Design" Philosophy

Unlike traditional loyalty programs, ProofVisit is built on the principle of data minimisation. Customer visit data is stored locally on the device, and we do not require accounts for standard users.

2. Information We Collect

A. Customer Data

All records of your visits and timestamps are stored exclusively in your device's local storage. We do not have access to this data.

B. Business Owner Data

We collect email addresses and business details necessary to manage your profile and subscription via Supabase Auth and StoreKit 2.

3. Data Storage and Security

Customer data is secured using the iOS Keychain and SwiftData for local encryption. Business data is synchronised securely via Supabase Cloud Storage.
